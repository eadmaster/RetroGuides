 
controls:

	d-pad = move
	X = jump (press again to double-jump) -> when jumping, check your landing point with the shadow
	[] = fires punches (Only two can be on screen at a time)
	/\ = stomp move (req. the battery gauge)
	O = use special item (in you have it in the upper left side of the screen, must be equipped on the map screen before each stage)
	R1 = gunsight, look around (standing still)

basics:

 - 30 of the first 46 stages must be cleared to access the final two stages.
 - clearing all stages gives you unlimited specials and fast recharge on stomps.
 - constantly check the remaning mission time
