## Trinea | Penta Dragon 2

controls (Sara):

	d-pad = move, aim
	A = focused attack
	B = wide attack
	Y = special attack (unlimited?)
	X = bomb-like attack (req. 1+ star in the bottom-right)
	half circle + B = dragon attack

basics:

 - press Select on the title screen to access the Configuration menu, where you can change the game difficulty.
 - easier char: Sara (has ranged attacks)
 - getting hit will the deplete the "P" gauge on the top-left. Visit certain NPCs to refill.
 - collect coins by killing enemies to spend at the shop (accessed every time a save is loaded or a gameover):
    - Life points +5 (gold cup)
    - Attack power +1 (gauntlet)
    - Bomb attack +1 (purple flask)
 - last area: ... (Sara scenario).
